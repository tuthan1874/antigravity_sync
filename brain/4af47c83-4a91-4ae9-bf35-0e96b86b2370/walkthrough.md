## Key Style Changes

### Colors
- **Primary**: `#FF9500` (Orange)
- **Primary Dark**: `#FF7A00`
- **Background**: `#0F0F0F`
- **Surface**: `#1A1A1A`
- **Text**: `#F2F2F2`

### Typography
- **Font**: Montserrat (Google Fonts)
- **Weights**: 400, 500, 600, 700, 800

### Effects Applied
- Orange glow shadows on buttons and cards
- Gradient primary buttons with hover animations
- Orange accent borders (1px solid rgba(255,149,0,0.2))
- Smooth transitions (0.3s ease)
